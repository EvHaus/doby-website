grid.events.scroll

Fired when the grid's viewport is scrolled.

Parameters

event (object) - Javascript event object

args (object) - This event's additional arguments.

Arguments

scrollLeft (integer) - The amount of pixels the viewport is currently scrolled horizontally.

scrollLeftDelta (integer) - The amount of pixels scrolled horizontally during this event.

scrollTop (integer) - The amount of pixels the viewport is currently scrolled vertically.

scrollTopDelta (integer) - The amount of pixels scrolled vertically during this event.

Example
new DobyGrid().on('scroll', function(event, args) {
    //console.log(event, args)
})
